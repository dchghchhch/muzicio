Who Are We

Our company, which goes by the name of MUZICIO, was established in 2023. We specialize in providing high-quality services and products to our clients in Music. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to ensuring that every client receives the best possible experience with our company. Whether you need assistance with a complex project or simply have a question about our services, we are always here to help. If you would like to get in touch with us, please don't hesitate to give us a massage . We look forward to hearing from you!

Frequently Asked Questions 

What is MUZICIO?

 MUZICIO is a music distribution and artist management company. We work with artists and labels to distribute and promote their music on major streaming platforms.

How can I apply to work with MUZICIO?

  To apply as an artist, please fill out our artist application form on our website. If you are a record label, please fill out our label application form.

What kind of artists do you work with?

 We work with artists in all genres of music, from pop and hip-hop to electronic and rock.

What services do you offer for artists?

We offer distribution services for your music on major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and Etc. We also offer artist management services, including marketing and promotion, social media management, and tour support.

What kind of deals do you offer for artists?

We offer both exclusive and non-exclusive distribution deals for artists. Our exclusive deals include additional promotion and marketing support.

 How long does it take to get my music on streaming platforms?

Once we receive your music and it is approved for distribution, it can take up to a week for your music to be available on streaming platforms.

 How do I get paid for my streams?

We pay our artists on a monthly basis for their streams, based on the revenue generated from streaming platforms.

What kind of support do you offer for touring?

We offer tour support for our artists, including booking, promotion, and logistics support.

 How can I get in touch with MUZICIO?

You can contact us through our website, or by emailing us at info@muzicio.com. We also have social media pages on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter where you can reach out to us.
